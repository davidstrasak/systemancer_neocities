My rating: <a href="https://www.goodreads.com/review/show/7693954124">5 of 5 stars</a><br /><br />
Amazing story with really good pacing and characters: 5/5<br /><br />This book is a journey to read through. It's easy to navigate, it's quick and made me feel very emotional about the characters and world in some moments. <br /><br />The book is even more special because I was a little nervous about the fact there are two other books that came earlier in the series.<br /><br />I bought this book because of the beautiful cover art and because of liking Tchaikowsky's other works. I haven't read those other entries into the series, but I feel that it was alright and did not require a lot of work to get up to speed with how this world works. I feel the story also does not have any direct ties to the previous books (it's just the same world).

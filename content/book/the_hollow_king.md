My rating: <a href="https://www.goodreads.com/review/show/6335963201">5 of 5 stars</a><br /><br />
Best age of sigmar book I've read so far.<br /><br />Compared to French's Ahriman series this one is definitely better - all the good points from the Ahriman books (like interesting complicated stories) remain, but the bad points (like story moving slowly or way too many plot lines) I did not notice here. <br /><br />The character of the hollow king is really cool and interesting. He's a somewhat heroic anti-hero, although he isn't even an anti-hero by choice, but rather by his race. <br /><br />The other characters were cool as well, the story featured a lot of twists and it was really swift and moved all over the place, but in a hood way, since I could keep up with it.

My rating: <a href="https://www.goodreads.com/review/show/7187733900">3 of 5 stars</a><br /><br />
Definitely an interesting read. The concept is cool, but it's all over the place - things appear to never be mentioned again and some other things are not that fleshed out. It had great ideas though, so that helps this book be interesting even with those flaws.<br /><br />The art is cool, but especially in the beginning really messy. When exploring the castle I can't really tell what am I looking at and what is happening. The art gets better later on, so it's not much of an issue.<br /><br />All in all, it really is what is being advertised - a cool looking comic with dark gods from before out time and their old worshippers. It's a quick read and the execution is done pretty well.

My rating: <a href="https://www.goodreads.com/review/show/6735285254">5 of 5 stars</a><br /><br />
What a story! This is one of the books that is really complex, will take you a long time to read, but once it's over you'll be sad.<br />The characters, the story, the world building, the hidden messages, the cool moments - all 5 stars. But it's all for the price that the book is sometimes hard to keep track of and understand what's going on.<br />The author knows the book is complex though, which is why there is a list of characters, list of factions and a map of the city of last chances.<br />All in all it is a book I had a hard time reading in the middle parts, but when I pushed through it I finished it in a relatively quick time for me.

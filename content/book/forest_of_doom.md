My rating: <a href="https://www.goodreads.com/review/show/6068419155">5 of 5 stars</a><br /><br />
A fun and nice gamebook that I really liked.<br />Unfortunately there is some luck needed at the start, but all in all you can pay some people to know where to go in the next run. And the next run even does not need to be done by a different character :D.<br />This game book is not even overly long or luck dependent, and it has a nice map that can show you which directions to take (although it can spoil some encounters), so overall a great read.

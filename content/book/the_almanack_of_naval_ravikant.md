My rating: <a href="https://www.goodreads.com/review/show/6023428083">4 of 5 stars</a><br /><br />
The Book is a good way to see into the mindset of a very successful investor. It has some good ideas and some nuggets of wisdom.<br />Whenever I've read it I've come across new and old things I was interested in - this book can be something like a signpost when you don't know what to do or what to learn about next.<br />That being said the editing really does not make sense in some places. <br />Some topics are spread out over the whole book in paragraphs instead of being confined to their own chapters. And some chapter titles are descriptive only of the first paragraph, while the rest of the chapter talks about different things.<br />Overall I think it's a good read if you're interested in this success mindset. It's free, so it's a good and available choice.

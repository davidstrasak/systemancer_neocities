My rating: <a href="https://www.goodreads.com/review/show/5730975671">5 of 5 stars</a><br /><br />
A nice, fun and interesting read where I was able to understand more than I though I would be to.<br />Stephen Hawking has a fun way of explaining the concepts and likes to make practical showcases of how the explained things could work. <br /><br />The book is pretty slim but still holds tons of info. The ending is pretty cool, because suddenly everything flows into itself and it all makes sense.<br /><br />And even at times when it didn't make sense, you can consult the dictionary at the end of the book to get reminded what the names like "Uncertainty principle" mean, if you forgot the explanation from before.<br /><br />The versions are also updated and have additional information since the first release date at the end. I liked that because it put into context some of the things that happened since the book was written.

My rating: <a href="https://www.goodreads.com/review/show/6676952014">5 of 5 stars</a><br /><br />
It's an amazing starter pack on decision making for everyone - be it personal decision making or corporate.<br />It explores themes from our default reactions keeping us from making decisions, then it goes to what is a decision and in the three quarters it really takes off with the practical guide to gathering evaluating and acting on information to improve the decision making process. It ends in a beautiful chapter on "how to find out what is it we want ", because you can make as good decisions as possible, but they will get you nowhere if you don't know where you're going. <br />It's really a book that makes you aware of all the fallacies which happen when deciding, so you can remember them and avoid them. A practical masterpiece - I did not think there would be so much useful information in this book and it exceeded my expectations.

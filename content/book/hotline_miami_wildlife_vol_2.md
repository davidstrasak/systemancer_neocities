My rating: <a href="https://www.goodreads.com/review/show/6583118482">1 of 5 stars</a><br /><br />
If you enjoy gore and cool art and don't care about anything else, it's good. If you expected a story, you're in the wrong place, because all you're getting is gore and depression (Everyone in this story is so depressed).<br /><br />After I read the first book, I had hoped that the second book would have more of a story, but what I found was even less story. It did not finish in an interesting way, it set up story points that went nowhere and the characters are stale and either one dimensional or unmotivated.<br /><br />There was one story point that I found interesting (the last issue of the comic series), but that ending was so disappointing that it just made me sad that this book exists.<br /><br />Not even the cool art saves this one. This is definitely the worst comic I have ever read and maybe even the worst book.

My rating: <a href="https://www.goodreads.com/review/show/5584460939">5 of 5 stars</a><br /><br />
The Book is an amazing read. It is actually a really gritty and dark warhammer story as expected.<br /><br />The book moved quickly from place to place without getting stale. It also has interesting characters and atmosphere. <br /><br />The only thing which could be considered bad is that it does not feel like much happened - rather feels like a setup to the trilogy.<br /><br />The book was fun and interesting though. Enough happened so the fact it might be a setup does not bother me. It was a good read.

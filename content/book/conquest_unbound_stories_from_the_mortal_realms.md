A great book full of amazing stories. The stories quality overall is very high, and I also liked how each story is only about 30 pages on average, so it can be really quick to read.<br />It is also interesting how since you don't know who the story is really about, the main established protagonist might not survive in some stories. Gives me the grimdark vibes :D.
